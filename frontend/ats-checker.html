ATS Optimization Tips

✅ Do's

  • • Use standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
  • • Include relevant keywords from job descriptions
  • • Use simple formatting and avoid tables
  • • Include all required sections (contact, experience, education)
  • • Use bullet points for easy scanning

❌ Don'ts

  • • Avoid images, graphics, or complex layouts
  • • Don't use headers or footers
  • • Avoid excessive formatting or colors
  • • Don't use abbreviations without explanation
  • • Avoid creative fonts or unusual formatting
